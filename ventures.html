At Rhino Ventures we aim to create a diversified portfolio of companies across different sectors, markets, and industries.

We believe that having one core development team building our ventures in the Vietnam office gives us the advantage of attracting the right kind of tech professionals at a sustainable remuneration, learning from each venture, sharing insights, modules, and best practices in areas possible.

This allows us an economy of scale while also being agile and able to quickly adapt to market changes by scaling up or down depending on market seasonality.

Our research and development office is housed in Da Nang whereas the off-working hours offer an unparalleled lifestyle between mountains, beaches and the ocean to ignite creativity on the work side. Our business office is based in Singapore, the heart and business center of South East Asia with a handful of seasoned executives frequently traveling the region and to the Vietnam office.

Our first venture is around pet well-being, MyPet, creating an eco-system for everything concerning a Pet’s life. The second venture is groundbreaking in the travel industry, The Point Exchange, a full-suite travel loyalty exchange platform to help the travel industry tackle the future of traveling in new ways post the COVID19 crisis.
