Company Profile

Our vision is to create at least one Rhino in 5 years

Rhino Ventures were born out of the common need by its two co-founders to build groundbreaking and sustainable tech-businesses.

On the one hand, one had sold his digital media business to a German conglomerate and was ready to embark on a new journey, the other had taken the helm of an industrial listed company and changed its course from its traditional roots to become a venture capital investment company. ​

They both agreed that valuations of tech companies were inflated in general with little to no original ideas and genuine entrepreneurs around. The scare tech talent in Asia was another big hurdle. Lastly, the constant chase of Unicorn status fueled by VC's and entrepreneurs alike, only to raise the next round albeit on a higher valuation often on the back of unsustainable hypergrowth with no proven product-market fit insight was another major industry problem.

They agreed that the right way to tackle this was to form a new kind of company, Rhino Ventures Global, that would focus on venture building; creating, building, launching, and owning the majority in the ventures as opposed to investing in external companies with minority positions and influence.​

Rhino Ventures was set out to launch 5 ventures in 24 months, creating ventures with genuine product-market fit achieving exponential organic growth. ​

At any given time Rhino Ventures will run 5 concurrent ventures as one comes to fruition and subsequently becomes independent another venture will be launched.

The joint vision is that one of the 5 ventures should become a Rhino (USD 1B valuation on an earnings multiple) in 5 years. We don't believe in fairytale Unicorn's but we do believe in smart, powerful, agile, and self-sustainable companies, just like Rhinos.

Rhino Ventures was built during one of the worst pandemics in modern history which has helped the founders to think about creating businesses that can thrive in good times as well as bad times.
