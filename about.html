Company Profile

Rhino Ventures was born out of the joint need by its founders. The original Rhino tribe of like-minded entrepreneurs to build groundbreaking and sustainable tech businesses with an Asia first approach.

The founders believe that tech start-ups, in general, are overvalued with little-to-no original ideas backed by real genuine entrepreneurs that decided to become entrepreneurs for the right reasons.

They believe becoming an entrepreneur, a word commonly misused in today's society, is not a trend but a true calling to solve everyday problems by innovative and new approaches.

Another common industry problem identified by the founders is the on-going chase of Unicorn status fuelled by VC's and entrepreneurs alike, only to raise the next round, albeit on a higher valuation often on the back of unsustainable hyper-growth with no proven product-market fit.

They agreed that the solution to the identified issues was to form a new kind of company that would focus on self-ideated and funded ventures. Essentially a venture-builder but with the difference of funding, building, launching, running, and owning the vast majority as opposed to VC investing in external companies with minority ownership and influence or the typical venture-building cum accelerator model.

Rhino Ventures set out to launch five ventures, to start with, creating ventures with genuine product-market fit achieving exponential organic growth.

At any given time, Rhino Ventures intend to run five concurrent ventures as one comes to fruition and subsequently becomes independent; another will come alive.

Rhino Ventures' vision is that one of the ventures should become a “Rhino” in 5 years, "Rhino" by tech definition: USD 1B valuation on an earnings multiple as opposed to revenue multiple. At Rhino Ventures, we don't believe in fairy-tale Unicorn's, but we do in talented, powerful, brave, agile, and self-sustainable ventures, just like the Rhinoceros.

Rhino Ventures launched during one of the worst pandemics in modern history, which has helped the founders to think about creating businesses that can thrive in all seasons.
