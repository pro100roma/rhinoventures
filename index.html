Who are Rhino Ventures

WHAT

Investing in self-started tech businesses as opposed to minority investing in external companies.

TYPE

Innovative tech, market-void-fillers, disruptive and some semi-synthetic ideas to be launched.

METHOD

Lean setup, proven entrepreneur advisors and professional executive team. Insight hacking, value hacking followed by growth hacking to properly achieve product market fit.

ADVANTAGE

Self-funded projects, majority owners, no outside influence, thus work with patience, allow artists to be artists, mitigate shortfalls with expert advisors and a professional exec-team, all with skin in the game.
